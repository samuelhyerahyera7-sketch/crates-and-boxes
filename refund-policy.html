Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Our Commitment

At Crates & Boxes, we take pride in the quality of our products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order and we will work with you to resolve the matter.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused and in its original condition
  • The item must be returned in its original packaging
  • You must contact us within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Proof of purchase must be provided

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or refunded:

  • Customised or branded products (foiled, printed, or personalised items)
  • Items that have been used, damaged after delivery, or altered
  • Items returned without prior authorisation

4. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage or incorrect product. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you.

5. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the outcome. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days via the original payment method or EFT, as agreed.

6. Return Shipping

Unless the return is due to our error (damaged or incorrect item), the cost of return shipping is the responsibility of the customer. We recommend using a trackable courier service as we cannot be held liable for items lost in transit.

7. Order Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled before they have been dispatched or prepared for collection. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel. Cancellations of custom/branded orders may not be possible once production has begun.

8. Consumer Rights

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008 (CPA). Where applicable, statutory rights under the CPA take precedence.
